ABOUT US.

Our Company was founded in 2016, after the founding members developed a scalable integrated inventory system for a local business in Mississauga. The company stems from seeing the need for robust system development, tailored to local businesses at an accessible price.

We support more than 30 small to medium sized business in the Greater Toronto Area, developing all sort of systems side-by-side with the people who will be using them. Ensuring the usability, and workflow is tailor-fitted to the client’s expectations. From developing Little Listro’s Veggie Market’s inventory system, to helping realize Canada Sees Travel Agency jump from a two-person operation to a 18-person endeavor, developing the company’s financial and scheduling system, we have maintained our close relationship with our clients to pinpoint the needs and deliver a better result.

Big Carrot Cooperative farmers was our biggest client, we developed their whole system. From developing the software to manage incoming products, facilities usage and distribution of income, to setting up server and contracting services, we were involved on the whole process. They are now operating with more than 30 different family-run farms, all of which have instant access to coop scheduling, financial reports, and distribution history. And we are happy to be part of their history.

WHY CHOOSE US.

Our Company excels in developing tailor-made software to assist businesses to achieve their goals, maintain their budgetary needs, and carry on focussing on their clients. We believe that businesses already have too much to worry about. Their internal systems should “just work”. Similarly to a tool it must be up to the task at hand, and not falter under any circumstances.

We are different from other companies because we work closely to understand what are the problems and needs of every stakeholder involved. We work on setting real expectations, so no surprises come when developing solutions, and we know requirement gathering is key to a great project outcome.
